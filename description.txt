en:

To summon the stand check the control settings.

This is a fan mod by JoJo Bizzare Adventure.

At the moment, the mod adds 5 stands:
Star Platinum, Hermit Purple, The World, Magician's Red and Hierophant Green
And also on some sets of armor, improving the characteristics of stand.
In the future, we plan to add new stands, new abilities and new clothes.

The arrow for the stand is issued at the beginning of the game.
For questions and suggestions, please contact our public in VK.
